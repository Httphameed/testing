Return Policy

At Luxu D'ouest, we strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you can return or exchange your item(s) under the following conditions:

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt.
  • Items must be in original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.
  • Sale items and gift cards are not eligible for returns.

Process for Returns

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@luxudouest.com to initiate a return.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  3. Our team will provide you with a return authorization number and return instructions.
  4. Pack the items securely in the original packaging (if possible).
  5. Include your return authorization number inside the package.
  6. Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service team.

Refunds

Once we receive your returned item(s) and inspect them, we will process your refund. Refunds will be credited to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days. Please note that shipping charges are non-refundable.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@luxudouest.com. Provide your order number and a photo of the damaged or defective item. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases where the item is damaged or defective, or an incorrect item was sent.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us at support@luxudouest.com
