Track Your Order

Thank you for shopping with Luxu D'ouest! You can track the status of your order here.

How to Track Your Order

To track your order, please follow these steps:

  1. Find order number in the confirmation email you received after placing your order.
  2. Enter order number and email address you used to place the order in the form below.
  3. Click on "Track Order" button to see the status of your shipment.
